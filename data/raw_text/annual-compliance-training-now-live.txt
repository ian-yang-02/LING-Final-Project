UPDATE: (Dec. 4, 2020) Due to technical issues with the learning management platform (D2L), the deadline to complete this year’s Annual Compliance Training has been extended to Dec. 14 at midnight ET.
*****************
Beginning today, all faculty, staff (including Tech Temps), and student employees hired prior to Oct. 1, 2020, may complete the 2020 Annual Compliance Training. This year, employees are required to complete the following four training modules:
USG Ethics Training.
Information Security/Cybersecurity Training.
Georgia Tech Foreign Influence, Research Integrity, and Conflict of Interest Training.
Conflict of Interest Attestation.
You must complete all four of the Compliance Training modules between Oct. 26 and Nov. 16. Employees who do not fully complete the requirements by midnight on Nov. 16 may receive a formal disciplinary notice from the Institute, and their access to Georgia Tech’s network may also be revoked.
The Annual Compliance Training can be completed on a desktop or laptop. It should take approximately 45 minutes, and it does not have to be completed at one time. Visit
training.hr.gatech.edu
today to complete the 2020 Annual Compliance Training.
For more information on the 2020 Annual Compliance Training, including helpful resources, visit
hr.gatech.edu/integrity-and-compliance .
