2021 Annual Compliance Training Ending Soon
All faculty, staff (including Tech Temps), and student employees hired prior to Oct. 1, 2021, are required to complete the 2021 Annual Compliance Training no later than Friday, Nov. 19.
Nov 16, 2021
— Atlanta, GA
All faculty, staff (including Tech Temps), and student employees hired prior to Oct. 1, 2021, are required to complete the 2021 Annual Compliance Training no later than Friday, Nov. 19. This year, employees are required to complete the following four training modules:
USG Ethics Training
Information Security/Cybersecurity Training
Georgia Tech Foreign Influence, Research Integrity, and Conflict of Interest Training
Conflict of Interest Attestation
You must complete all four of the Compliance Training modules by Nov. 19. Employees who do not fully complete the requirements by midnight on Nov. 19 may receive a formal disciplinary notice from the Institute.
The Annual Compliance Training can be completed on a desktop or laptop. It should take approximately 45 minutes, and it does not have to be completed at one time. Visit
Georgia Tech’s Employee Learning Portal
(powered by Genius) today to complete the 2021 Annual Compliance Training.
For more information on the 2021 Annual Compliance Training, including helpful resources, visit
hr.gatech.edu/integrity-and-compliance .
