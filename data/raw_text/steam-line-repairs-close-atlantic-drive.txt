Steam Line Repairs Close Atlantic Drive
Starting July 13, Atlantic Drive will be temporarily shut down for steam line repairs.

Due to necessary steam line repairs, Atlantic Drive will be temporarily shutdown beginning July 13 through October 31, 2015. The repairs are required to improve the dedicated steam line that provides heating and pressure to many buildings on west campus. The road will remain pedestrian-accessible during this construction project. For more information on this and other construction projects on campus, visit the Facilities Management Design and Construction website
