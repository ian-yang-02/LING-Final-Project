The Kendeda Building Awarded Highest Honor for Design and Sustainability
The Kendeda Building for Innovative Sustainable Design – the first Living Building Challenge 3.1 certified building in the Southeast – receives national American Institute of Architect’s (AIA) Honor Award for Architecture.
Spring flowers outside The Kendeda Building. Photo by Allison Carter.
Mar 03, 2022
— Atlanta, GA
The Kendeda Building for Innovative Sustainable Design – the first Living Building Challenge 3.1 certified building in the Southeast – receives national American Institute of Architect’s (AIA) Honor Award for Architecture. This award celebrates the best architecture that highlights the many ways buildings and spaces can improve our lives. Read the announcement
