below the belt 
 fletcher moore pilots his commuter bike along the entirety of the incomplete beltline. 
 fletcher moore 
 aug 14, 2015 
 — atlanta, ga 
 on a scorching day in early july 2015, georgia tech employee and author, fletcher moore, piloted his commuter bike along the entirety of the incomplete beltline. what he found was a “study in contrasts: the devastated atlanta of the past and the atlanta of the future.”  watch his gopro video and read his essay about the trek:  below the belt
