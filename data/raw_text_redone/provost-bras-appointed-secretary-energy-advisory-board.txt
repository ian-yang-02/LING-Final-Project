provost bras appointed to secretary of energy advisory board 
 rafael bras joins independent advisory committee for u.s. energy secretary moniz. 
 aug 26, 2013 
 — atlanta, ga 
 the u.s. department of energy today announced georgia tech provost rafael bras has been appointed to the secretary of energy advisory board (seab). the nineteen member board comprised of scientists, business executives, academics and former government officials will serve as an independent advisory committee to energy secretary moniz.
