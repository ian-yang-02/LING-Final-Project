 all faculty, staff (including tech temps), and student employees hired prior to oct. 1, 2020, must complete the 2020 annual compliance training by midnight (et) monday, december 14. this year, employees are required to complete the following four training modules: 
 usg ethics training. 
 information security / cybersecurity training. 
 georgia tech foreign influence, research integrity, and conflict of interest training. 
 conflict of interest attestation. 
 employees who do not fully complete the requirements by midnight on dec. 14 may receive a formal disciplinary notice from the institute, and their access to georgia tech’s network may also be revoked. 
 the annual compliance training can be completed on a desktop or laptop. it should take approximately 45 minutes, and it does not have to be completed at one time. visit 
 training.hr.gatech.edu 
 today to complete the 2020 annual compliance training. 
 for more information on the 2020 annual compliance training, including helpful resources, visit 
 hr.gatech.edu / integrity-and-compliance .
