university system of georgia announces one-time bonuses coming this spring 
 chancellor steve wrigley of the university system of georgia (usg) announced that 
 full-time system employees making less than $80,000 a year will receive a one-time $1,000 bonus this spring. 
 feb 15, 2021 
 — atlanta, ga 
 last friday afternoon, chancellor steve wrigley of the university system of georgia (usg) announced that in alignment with a recent announcement made by the governor, lieutenant governor, and speaker of the house, full-time system employees making less than $80,000 a year will receive a one-time $1,000 bonus this spring. 
 “we are delighted that theses bonuses are being extended to usg employees,” said president ángel cabrera. “we are grateful for our hard-working employees and their commitment during these challenging times.” 
 more details will be provided once they become available.
