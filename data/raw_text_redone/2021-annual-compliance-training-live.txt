 oct 25, 2021 
 — atlanta, ga 
 beginning today, all faculty, staff (including tech temps), and student employees hired prior to oct. 1, 2021, may complete the 2021 annual compliance training. this year, employees are required to complete the following four training modules: 
 usg ethics training 
 information security / cybersecurity training 
 georgia tech foreign influence, research integrity, and conflict of interest training 
 conflict of interest attestation 
 you must complete all four of the compliance training modules between oct. 25 and nov. 19. employees who do not fully complete the requirements by midnight on nov. 19 may receive a formal disciplinary notice from the institute. 
 the annual compliance training can be completed on a desktop or laptop. it should take approximately 45 minutes, and it does not have to be completed at one time. visit 
 georgia tech’s employee learning portal 
 (powered by genius) today to complete the 2021 annual compliance training. 
 for more information on the 2021 annual compliance training, including helpful resources, visit 
 hr.gatech.edu / integrity-and-compliance .
